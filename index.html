ITEMS I WOULD LIKE TO COVER:

For clarification and suggestions.

HOMEWORK - What we generally expected from students?

  1. Items specifically required by the assignment.
  2. Logically assumed: students apply what was learned in class:
    • Good organization (folder classification, etc)
    • A clear visual display (readable items, no side-ups, etc)
    • Content management (no irrelevant files, etc).
    • Proper labels/titles.
  3. Given for granted even before students enter the program:
    • Acceptable spelling and grammar.
    • Time management.
    • Knowledge complementation (google, reading, etc)

GRADING - What we want to accomplish?

  1. Learning Indicator. All deliverables of the individual component must be coherent with the material learned in class. A list of items delivered randomly without intention, but only the purpose of the meeting listed requirements should not be sufficient for a very high grade.
    • Student demonstrates comprehension by creating unified whole deliverables that show an understanding of the process.
  2. Quality. How should we grade?
    • Favor mastery of the content learned rather than a percentage of items delivered.
    • Praise the right mindset, creativity and UX approach over skillful item completion.
